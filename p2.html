Tourist Attraction

Eram Garden
a historical garden in the city of Shiraz, which has been registered as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Iranian garden design and lush trees and various flowers.

Golestan Palace collection of palaces and gardens in Tehran that belongs to the Qajar era. Beautiful architecture and artistic mirror work.

Persepolis
The capital of the Achaemenid Empire and one of the most important ancient sites in the world. Magnificent architecture with tall columns.

Ali Sadr Cave
One of the largest water caves in the world is in Hamedan, and boating in the clear waters is a sight to behold stalagmites and stalactites.

Sheikh Lotfollah Mosque
One of the most beautiful mosques in the world is located in Isfahan. Exquisite tiling and unique dome.

Kish Island
A touristic island in the Persian Gulf with beautiful beaches and water sports, shopping centers and nightlife.
