Travel Tips

Traveling to Iran is a rich and diverse experience that can include culture, history, nature, and hospitality of the people.
Below are some important points of traveling to Iran:

🌐Planning before the trip:
- Research the destination: gather information about culture, attractions, weather and local laws.
- Hotel and ticket reservation: Plan in advance for accommodation and transportation.

🌐Proper packaging:
- Clothing suitable for the weather: choose clothes suitable for the weather conditions of the destination.
- Necessary items: Don't forget medicines, toiletries, and electronics.

🌐Shipping:
- Getting to know the public transport system: get information about local buses, subways or taxis.
- Car rental: If you plan to travel by car, check the rental conditions.

🌐Respect for local culture:
- Customs: Get to know local customs and respect them.
- Language: learning a few words of the local language can be useful.

🌐Use of technology:
-Maps and applications: use navigation and translation applications.
- Social networks: Help others by sharing your experiences.
